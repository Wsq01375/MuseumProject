This is the home page! Welcome!

Welcome to Unwrapped, a curated site for art lovers. This site"unwraps" the unfamiliar and the unexpected. For those seeking a fresh art experience, we hope our site will rekindle your love of art and travel. Here you can glean information about unusual art, rarely known art museums, and suggestions for accommodations and dining. For those who are experiencing the art world for the first time, our site will inspire you with the power of creativity in different countries, ethnic associations, and religions. We invite you to chart your own path below and unwrap your next art adventure!
